![[PotatoPottingMixBudget.png]]

Mix in a few handfuls of [[Blood Fish & Bone]]
For determinate varieties (tubers on one layer) put two seed potatoes at 1/3rd up (sprinkle in a small handful of [[Blood Fish & Bone]] and gently mix into the top inch), then another two, at 90 degrees, at 2/3rds up (again sprinkle in the [[Blood Fish & Bone]]).

For indeterminate varieties, just put two seed potatoes at the 1/3rd up layer (sprinkle in the [[Blood Fish & Bone]]).
