## General Notes
Notes:

- One of the most blight-resistant potato varieties available.
- Late maincrop variety producing very heavy yields.
- Large oval tubers with red skin and white flesh.
- Excellent choice for organic growing due to its exceptional disease resistance.
- Continues growing strongly even when other varieties succumb to late blight.
- Produces large crops suitable for feeding a family.
- Good all-round kitchen potato.
- Excellent for roasting, baking, mashing and chips.
- Stores exceptionally well through winter when properly cured.
- Vigorous plants require more space than many other potato varieties.
- One of the best choices for the UK's increasingly unpredictable summers.
