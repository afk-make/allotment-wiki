## 📌 Extra Details


- Classic globe beetroot and one of the most dependable traditional varieties.
- Produces smooth, round to slightly oval roots with deep maroon-red flesh.
- Often sold as **Detroit 2 Crimson Globe**, an improved form of the original Detroit beetroot.
- Renowned for excellent flavour, texture and reliability.
- Suitable for harvesting young as baby beetroot or left to mature into larger roots.
- Dense flesh gives excellent cooking quality and storage potential.
- Performs well across a wide range of soil types and weather conditions.
- Heat tolerant and widely adapted.
- Stores well both in the ground and after lifting.
- Excellent for roasting, pickling, salads and winter storage.
- Direct sowing is generally preferred, though early indoor sowing is possible.

Why Grow It:

- Outstanding flavour.
- Reliable cropping.
- Excellent storage.
- Dense, high-quality roots.
- Suitable for fresh eating and preserving.
- One of the best traditional beetroot varieties available.

Many allotment growers regard Detroit 2 as one of the finest all-round beetroot varieties. If Boltardy is the dependable early beetroot, Detroit 2 is the variety I'd choose for flavour, storage and a long harvesting season.
