## 📌 Extra Details

Notes:

- One of the most popular salad potato varieties grown in the UK.
- Produces smooth, long oval tubers with pale yellow skin and creamy yellow flesh.
- Renowned for its outstanding flavour and waxy texture.
- Holds its shape exceptionally well after cooking.
- Excellent for boiling, steaming, potato salads and roasting.
- One of the few potatoes that is equally good eaten hot or cold.
- High yielding and reliable in UK conditions.
- Good resistance to common scab and mechanical damage.
- Performs well in containers as well as open ground.
- Can be harvested early as "new potatoes" or left slightly longer for larger tubers.
- RHS Award of Garden Merit variety.

Growing Cycle:

```
Mar-Apr
Plant seed potatoes
```

Container Growing:

- Excellent container variety.
- Recommended container size: **30–40 litres**.
- Plant **2 seed potatoes** per 30-litre container.
- Earth up stems as they grow.
- Keep evenly watered, especially while tubers are forming.

Why Grow It:

- Outstanding flavour.
- One of the best salad potatoes available.
- Excellent in containers.
- Reliable and productive.
- Perfect for summer eating.
- Great choice for late planting in July for an autumn crop in containers.

For your allotment, Charlotte complements Sarpo Mira perfectly. Sarpo Mira gives you heavy winter storage crops, while Charlotte provides superb-quality new potatoes through the summer and is also one of the best candidates for your idea of growing a second crop in 30-litre containers for an autumn harvest.
