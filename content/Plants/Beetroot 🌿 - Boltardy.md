## 📌 Extra Details


- One of the most popular beetroot varieties grown in the UK.
- RHS Award of Garden Merit (AGM) variety.
- Famous for its excellent resistance to bolting, making it ideal for early sowings.
- Produces smooth, round, deep red roots with good internal colour.
- Reliable in a wide range of UK growing conditions.
- Suitable for harvesting young as baby beetroot or left to mature for larger roots.
- Excellent for roasting, salads, pickling and storage.
- One of the best beginner beetroot varieties.
- Multigerm seed variety, meaning each seed cluster can produce several seedlings and may require thinning.
- Successional sowings every few weeks can provide harvests throughout summer and autumn.

Why Grow It:

- Extremely reliable.
- Excellent bolt resistance.
- Good for early sowing.
- Suitable for pickling.
- Easy to grow.
- Perfect for succession sowing.

For a Worcestershire allotment, if I were only growing one beetroot variety, Boltardy would be very difficult to beat. It's reliable, productive, beginner-friendly and one of the few beetroot varieties that copes particularly well with early sowings without rushing to seed.
