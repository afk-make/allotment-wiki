A couple of notes specific to **Invicta**:

- Very vigorous and productive.
- Large pale-green berries.
- One of the most mildew-resistant gooseberries.
- Generally considered an **early to mid-season** variety.
- Quite thorny (worth noting in the body of the note if not as a property).
- Excellent for cooking when slightly under-ripe, but can also be eaten fresh when fully ripe.
