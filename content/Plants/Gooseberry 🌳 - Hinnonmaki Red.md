Notes:

- Produces medium-sized deep red berries.
- Widely regarded as one of the best dessert gooseberries.
- Sweeter than Invicta when fully ripe.
- Compact and manageable bush, making it well suited to allotments and smaller gardens.
- Good cold hardiness (developed in Finland).
- Can be eaten fresh straight from the bush or used in desserts and jams.
- Usually crops heavily and reliably once established.
- Thorny, though generally less vigorous than Invicta.
- Often ripens slightly earlier than Invicta in favourable seasons.
