Notes:

Sow SEEDS densely

- Traditional small white onion grown specifically for pickling.
- Produces compact, uniform bulbs ideal for jars.
- Usually grown at much closer spacing than standard onions to encourage small bulb size.
- One of the classic varieties used for homemade pickled onions.
- Can also be used fresh in stews, casseroles and roasting when harvested young.
- White-skinned bulbs develop a crisp texture and clean onion flavour.
- Quick to mature compared with many storage onions.
- Often harvested earlier than maincrop onions.
- Easy to grow and highly productive in a small space.
- Particularly suitable for gardeners who enjoy preserving their harvest.

Growing Method:
Sow or plant densely
        ↓
Small bulbs develop
        ↓
Harvest Jul-Aug
        ↓
Pickle whole


Role in Dad's Onion Collection: 
```
Radar             → Early onions
Setton            → Main cooking onions
Yellow Rynsburger → Long-storage onions
Red Baron         → Red onions
Silverskin        → Pickling onions
```


Unlike Sturon or Yellow Rynsburger, where the goal is large storage bulbs, Silverskin is grown specifically for quantity of small bulbs. If your dad enjoys making pickled onions, this is probably the most rewarding onion variety in the collection because even a short row can fill a surprising number of jars.
