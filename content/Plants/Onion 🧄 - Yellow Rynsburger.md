Notes:

- Traditional Dutch storage onion renowned for exceptional keeping qualities.
- Produces medium to large round bulbs with golden-yellow skins.
- One of the classic long-storage onions grown throughout northern Europe.
- Particularly valued for winter use due to its ability to remain firm and usable for many months after harvest.
- Stronger flavour than many modern supermarket onions.
- Excellent for soups, stews, curries, roasting and caramelising.
- High yielding and reliable in UK conditions.
- Suitable for growing from seed, seedlings or sets.
- Benefits from a long growing season and good curing before storage.
- A favourite among growers seeking self-sufficiency through the winter months.
- Often chosen specifically for storage performance rather than exhibition-size bulbs.
