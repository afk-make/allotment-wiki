Notes:

- Modern maincrop onion widely regarded as the successor to Sturon.
- Excellent all-round cooking onion.
- Produces large, round, golden-brown bulbs.
- One of the most reliable onion varieties for UK allotments.
- Outstanding storage ability; bulbs can often keep until spring when stored correctly.
- Heavy cropping and very uniform.
- Suitable for growing from seed or sets.
- Good disease resistance and strong field performance.
- Particularly useful for households that cook with onions frequently.
- Ideal variety for soups, stews, curries, roasting and general kitchen use.
- Often recommended as a "grow every year" onion by experienced allotment holders.
