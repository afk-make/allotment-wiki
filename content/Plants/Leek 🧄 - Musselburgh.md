## 📌 Extra Details

Harvest Timeline

Together [[Leek 🧄 - Bandit]], [[Leek 🧄 - Musselburgh]], and [[Leek 🧄 - Giant Bulgarian]] varieties give:

```
Sep-Dec  Giant BulgarianNov-Mar  MusselburghJan-Apr  Bandit
```

So with a single sowing period:

```
Jan-Apr  SowMay-Jul  Plant outSep-Apr  Harvest
```

you can have fresh leeks available for roughly **8 months of the year**, which is one of the reasons they're such a valuable allotment crop. In your vault, these three would make an excellent "reference leek collection" in the same way you've chosen French Breakfast, Watermelon, and Black Spanish Round for radishes.
