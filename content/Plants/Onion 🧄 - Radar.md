Notes:

- The most widely grown overwintering onion set in the UK.
- Planted in autumn and harvested the following summer.
- Provides one of the earliest bulb onion harvests of the gardening year.
- Extremely hardy and capable of surviving severe winter weather.
- Produces medium to large golden-brown bulbs.
- Particularly useful for bridging the gap before spring-sown onions mature.
- Reliable and beginner-friendly.
- Usually grown exclusively from sets.
- Requires very little attention through winter once established.
- One of the easiest ways to achieve an early onion crop.
- Harvests often begin several weeks before Sturon, Yellow Rynsburger and Red Baron.

Why Grow It:

- Earliest onions of the year.
- Very reliable in UK conditions.
- Excellent use of otherwise empty winter bed space.
- Allows fresh home-grown onions months before the maincrop harvest.
- Pairs perfectly with storage varieties such as Sturon and Yellow Rynsburger.
