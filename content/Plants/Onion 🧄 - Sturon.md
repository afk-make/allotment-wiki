Notes:

- Traditional yellow storage onion and one of the most widely grown allotment varieties.
- Considered the benchmark against which many modern storage onions are compared.
- Produces round to globe-shaped straw-coloured bulbs.
- Excellent storage potential and can keep for many months when properly cured.
- Reliable cropping with strong resistance to bolting.
- RHS Award of Garden Merit variety.
- Suitable for growing from seed, but most UK growers use sets.
- High yielding and well suited to UK conditions.
- Particularly popular among allotment growers because of its consistency and ease of growing.
- Good flavour for general cooking, roasting, soups and stews.
- Often recommended as a beginner-friendly onion variety.
